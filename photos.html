Photography

Travel photography from backpacking through South-East Asia. In the fall of 2016 I travelled from Canada to South East Asia for 3 months where I travelled by motorbike up the South coast of Vietnam, slept on buses all the way through Cambodia and night-trained my way to Southern Thailand. For my journeys I shot photos along the way on my Nikon D5100.
